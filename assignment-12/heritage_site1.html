About Heritage Group of Monuments at Hampi

Welcome to Heritage Hampi .Hampi is located in hilly terrain formed by granite boulders The Hampi monuments comprising the UNESCO world heritage site are a subset of the wider-spread Vijayanagara ruins. Almost all of the monuments were built between 1336 and 1570 CE during the Vijayanagara rule.

History

Hampi was founded in the middle of the 14th century by two princes named Hakka and Bukka. Most of the temples in the Hampi district were built between 1336 and 1570 CE during the Vijayanagara empire.

Architectural Marvel

The entire temple complex is built in Dravidian style of architecture with high walls of compound and watch towers around. Many halls, shrines, pavilions all are made of stone. The Maha Mantap is set inside the complex.
